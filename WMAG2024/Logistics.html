Accommodation
June is high season in Varenna. BnB and hotels are expected to get fully booked by the end of January. For this reason, it is crucial that participants book their accommodation as soon as possible.

We suggest BnB and apartments since they usually provide best value for money. You can find a list here: https://varennaturismo.com/en/hotel-bb-and-rooms/ or through the usual websites (airbnb, etc.).       

There are also a few hotels. Luxury hotels are usually booked years in advance. There are also a few mid-range and budget hotels. Here is a list of these hotels within walking distance from the conference venue:

Albergo Milano (mid-range, best option)
Hotel Montecodeno (budget)
Hotel Beretta (budget)
Albergo Ristorante del Sole (budget)

If you have or planning to have your own wheels, there are of course plenty of other options in the area around Varenna. In particular, we suggest the following:

Hotel il Portichetto in the village of Vezio. This village is right on top of the hill above Varenna with fantastic views of the lake. There is a hiking path which goes from Varenna to Vezio, so in principle one can hike back and forth to Villa Monstero in about 15-20 minutes, but the path is steep and it can get very hot in June, so we still suggest to consider this option only if you have a car.

Agriturismo Castello di Vezio: Agriturismo Castello di Vezio . Again in Vezio, see above.

Fiumelatte: this is the village right south of Varenna with more B&Bs and Apartments, some of them are walking distance from Villa Monastero but the others might require a few minutes drive by car.
Bellano: 10-min drive on the northern side of Varenna, it has a similar number of accommodation options as Varenna. Have a look here: Discovering Bellano
